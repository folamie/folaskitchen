FOLAS KITCHEN

Established by chef and founder Adefola in 2016 after his tour to Spain,Folas kitchen has grown to include restaurants in Lagos, New York City,Toronto, Los Angeles and South Africa. The restaurant have gained world-wide recognition for their innovative take on cuisine, while supporting local, sustainable and reasponsible farmers and food purveyors.
